10am snack
~25g
Cottage cheese
1 cup cottage cheese: ~25g · Casein protein — digests slowly, delivers amino acids across a long window.
Highest-leverage change in the plan. No prep, no thought required. Add hot sauce or everything bagel seasoning if plain gets boring.
Lunch · ~12–1pm
~15g
Rice + Indian or frozen meal
Weakest link — limited control when eating out.
If frozen meals at home: read labels, target 20g+ protein options (Factor, Trader Joe's, etc).
If Indian out: bias toward chicken/paneer dishes over rice-heavy plates.

Dinner rule: Look at the plate. No obvious protein source → evening shake. 30 seconds, 21g, done.
Dinner rule: No protein visible on the plate = shake. Always. Penne pesto, rice dishes, and pasta nights are the main offenders. One shake fixes it.
