## Daily Work (every day including rest days)
**Mobility — every day:**
- Doorframe pec stretch: 30 sec/side, every doorframe pass at home. Fixes pec minor restriction causing shoulder rounding.
- Band pull-aparts: 20–30 reps, 1 set, morning or desk break. Rear delt + mid-trap activation.
- Hip flexor wall stretch: 60–90 sec/side, morning. Unlocks glute activation for every session.
- Goblet squat GTG: 5 reps/pass with 12kg at home whenever walking past the bell.

**Off days only (hall bar at home):**
- Pullup GTG: 1–3 reps every pass, neutral grip, never to failure, never when arms are tired.
- Dead hang: 20–30 sec after pullup sets. Best passive elbow rehab tool available.

**KB session days:**
- Warmup only. No pullup GTG on KB days — elbow protection trumps volume.
- Daily mobility still applies, do it earlier in the day.

## Shoulder Rounding Protocol
- Wife observes rounding — consistent with last KB cycle. She is the biofeedback tool.
- Check in with wife every 2 weeks.
- Fix is pull volume + pec mobility, not more pressing.
- Band pull-aparts and pullup GTG are non-negotiable until she stops noticing it.
- Expected resolution: 2wk wife notices improvement → 4wk pullups easier in session → 6–8wk structural change.
