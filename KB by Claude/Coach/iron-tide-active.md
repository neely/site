## Program Structure
**Odd weeks (1,3,5,7):** A / B / A
**Even weeks (2,4,6,8):** B / A / B

### Day A — Tide
ABC complex (2 cleans → 1 press → 3 FSQ) EMOM + farmer carry finisher
- EMOM interval per week schedule below
- Carries: 6 sets total, 3 each arm. **Suitcase first, rack carry second** (flipped Week 3 — rack carry burns forearms when done first)
- Carries are non-negotiable even when smoked from ABC

### Day B — Ember
1. Press ladders (double 16kg): 2-3-5 rungs
2. Half snatches (adjustable bell): 8/side per set, wrist neutral at catch always
3. Push/pull superset: pushups immediately → pullups, rest 90s between rounds. Stop 2 reps before failure.

## Active Coaching Calls
*These override the standard program targets above.*

**PRESS — HELD AT 30 REPS (3 STRICT LADDERS) THROUGH WEEK 4**
Jerk/thruster appearing consistently on ladder 3 rung 3. Rest times creeping on final 5s. Do not advance to 4 ladders until all 3 ladders are strict with consistent rest times. This applies Week 4 regardless of how good he feels.

**CARRIES — SUITCASE FIRST, RACK SECOND**
Rack carries after ABC complex burns forearms and accumulates toward medial elbow. Suitcase first while fresh, rack second.

**PULLUPS — NEUTRAL GRIP ONLY**
Supinated grip loads medial epicondyle. Tested and confirmed — neutral grip is correct for this athlete. No exceptions.

**SNATCH BELL**
Using adjustable at 16kg. Near failure on last 1-2 reps of set 3. Expected at this stage — watch catch mechanics at failure threshold. If true failure mid-set, rack and rest 15-20 seconds, finish reps. Do not drop to 12kg yet.

**PUSH/PULL RATIO**
Push volume significantly exceeds pull volume each session. Band pull-aparts and GTG pullups on off days are non-negotiable for shoulder rounding correction. Check wife's observation every 2 weeks.
